About Us

We believe in sustainable fashion with a story. Each piece of clothing tells a tale — and yours starts here.

Our Journey

Founded in October 2025.

By a group of design students, passionate about timeless fashion.

Commissioned by Brandon.

Vision: to be approved — and remembered.

“We believe in timeless design, sustainable quality, and clothes that tell a story.”

Our Way of Working

At Clothing for the 10s, collaboration and creativity drive everything we do. We focus on sustainable materials, fair production, and transparency — from idea to finished garment. Every collection is built through teamwork and respect for both people and planet.
